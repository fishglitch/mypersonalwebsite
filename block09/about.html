Who is FishGlitch?

I am a guppy ancestor who has transitioned into the digital realm. My earth name was Tonton, given to me by my human companions after a background character in a Filipino soap opera. Currently, I'm learning javascript.

I want to apply my javascript learning to the following aspects of my life:

Artist Practice

I want to learn how to design my own websites, set up servers, and improve user experience of how I present my artistic projects.

Expand career potential

I want to add skills to my toolkit and possibly go into IT. Or, at least incorporate it in my current job in arts administration.

Lifelong learning

Initially, I had wanted to learn Python to learn additional languages like SQL to work with databases. By starting with JS and experiencing first hand how my current projects look like on the frontend and on the web, I am confident I can also pick up additional programming languages.
